ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Chrishanke, a South African tattoo artist with a passion for bringing ink to life. Ever since I was 16, I've dreamed of transforming skin into art. After completing my Fine and Applied Arts degree at Tshwane University of Technology, I turned my dream into reality. I specialize in a variety of styles, but I'm particularly drawn to the timeless beauty of black and gray realism. Let's bring your tattoo vision to life!
